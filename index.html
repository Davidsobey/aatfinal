Our Services

What We Do

Vehicle Leasing

Our leases run for a minimum of 12 months. Mileage restrictions apply. Upfront deposit and management fees are applicable. Maintenance costs are for lessee account. Tracking devices and comprehensive insurance are included in the fee.
Lease to own options are available upon request, mileage restrictions are relaxed. Deposit and management fee applicable. Maintenance costs are for lessee account.

Vehicle Take-over

If you are no longer in a position to pay your finance company for your vehicle, AAT Auto Assist will facilitate the transfer of this responsibility to a new pre-approved user who will then pay the monthly instalment, insurance and tracker on your vehicle. Once the vehicle is settled ownership will be transferred to the lessee.
